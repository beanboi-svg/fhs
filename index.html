Enrollment Information

Frederick High School

Frederick High School, known for its leadership in dual and concurrent enrollment, offers students a variety of opportunities, including the Biomedical Science Academy, P-TEACH, P-TECH, a wide range of athletics and elective courses, and Advanced Placement (AP) coursework. These programs enable students to earn college credits while engaging in rigorous learning experiences.

St. Vrain Valley Schools Achieves the Highest Graduation Rate in Denver Metro Area

St. Vrain Valley Schools has achieved the highest on-time graduation rate in the district’s history, at 93.3%. This is also the highest graduation rate of any district in the Denver Metro area, and the highest of any district in Colorado with more than 300 graduates.
